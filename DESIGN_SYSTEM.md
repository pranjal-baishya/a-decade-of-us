# DESIGN SYSTEM

## Palette

Background:
- Deep midnight blue
- Soft black
- Dark navy gradients

Accent:
- Warm gold
- Rose gold
- Soft blush pink

Text:
- Warm ivory
- Soft white
- Muted gray

---

# Typography

Headers:
Elegant serif-like typography.

Body:
Clean sans-serif.

Mood:
Cinematic and emotional.

---

# Motion Rules

Animations should:
- be slow
- feel intentional
- feel cinematic
- avoid chaotic movement

Preferred animation types:
- fade
- glow
- drift
- scale
- parallax
- soft spring motion

---

# UI Style

Cards:
- glassmorphism
- soft borders
- subtle blur
- rounded corners
- warm glow

Spacing:
- generous whitespace
- breathable layouts

---

# Mobile Rules

- single-column layouts first
- large touch targets
- readable typography
- minimal GPU-heavy effects
- simplified 3D on mobile

---

# Visual Inspirations

Mood references:
- Interstellar
- Apple cinematic pages
- luxury photo journals
- Netflix documentary intros

---

# Interaction Philosophy

Interactions should feel:
- emotional
- intimate
- calm
- tactile

NOT:
- game-like
- noisy
- overwhelming
